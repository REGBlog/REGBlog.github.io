How To Play WordWrap

1. Guess the WordWrap word in 6 tries.

2. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

3. The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples:

R

: R is in the word and in the correct spot.

S

: S is in the word but in the wrong spot.

E

: E is not in the word in any spot.
